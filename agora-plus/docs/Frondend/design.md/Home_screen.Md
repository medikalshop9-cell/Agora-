# Design Document: AGORA+ Home Screen ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_2}}) 

## 1. Objective & Purpose
The Home Screen serves as the central jumping-off point for the AGORA+ application. Its primary goal is to provide UM6P club members with immediate access to critical updates, upcoming events, and quick actions, fostering a sense of community and active participation. 

## 2. Visual Strategy & Aesthetic
Following the **Agora Academic Modern** design system, the aesthetic is "The Digital Academy" — professional, clean, and scholarly yet modern. 

- **Color Palette:** Dominated by *Deep Blue (#1D3557)* for authority and *Gold* accents for calls to action, maintaining high contrast against a neutral off-white background. 
- **Typography:** Uses *Manrope* (as per the design system) or *Roboto* (as per the PRD) for a clean, sans-serif look that prioritizes readability. 
- **Card-Based Layout:** Information is modularized into cards with subtle shadows and `ROUND_EIGHT` corner roundness to create a structured, organized feel. 

## 3. Key Layout Sections
- **Top App Bar:** Branding-focused with the "AGORA+" logo and a notification badge for urgent alerts. 
- **Personalized Hero:** A friendly "Welcome back, [Name]" greeting to enhance user connection. 
- **Announcements Carousel:** Horizontal scroll of critical club updates (e.g., Q3 Case Study Competition), allowing users to scan news quickly. 
- **Quick Actions:** High-priority touch targets for "RSVP to Next Event" and "Join Meeting," designed as prominent tiles for 1-tap access. 
- **Upcoming Highlights:** A featured, high-contrast card for the most significant upcoming event (e.g., McKinsey Workshop), providing direct "Reserve My Seat" functionality. 
- **Bottom Navigation:** Persistent access to Home, Events, Community, Voting, and Profile. 

## 4. Design Principles Applied
- **Minimalism:** No unnecessary clutter; every element serves a functional purpose. 
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Large, bold titles for sections and distinct visual weighting for primary actions (buttons) vs. secondary information (timestamps). 
- **Mobile-First:** Optimized for one-handed use with primary touch targets in the lower and middle regions of the screen. 
- **Consistency:** Uses shared components (TopAppBar, BottomNavBar) to ensure a familiar experience across the entire app suite.
